Government Techonology Forum

Smart City, E-Government, Identity Management, Logistics, Revenue Management, Facility Management, Logistics in the public sector, are amongst the emerging concepts in the technology for governance domain, world over. For growing nations like Nigeria, the puzzle about these trends hinges on when and how should technology intercept with governance without disrupting the essence of governance and citizens’ well-being. The government technology forum will pull together government executives, policymakers, governance tech experts and models to explore these concerns and many more within the framework of delivering tech-driven solutions for government efficiency and citizens’ well-being.
