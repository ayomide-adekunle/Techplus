C-SUITE FORUM; (CEO, CIO, CTO, CFO)

In a relaxed inspiring atmosphere, this event with three (3) streams; The CEO track, The CIO/CTO track and the CMO track, will bring companies large and small from around the world to meet and learn how enterprise is evolving to meet the needs of a digital generation and modern business. For all CIOs, this will be an excellent opportunity to engage your CEO and key executives outside the office in a relaxed inspiring environment that will make difficult technical conversations easy.
